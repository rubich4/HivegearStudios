WHAT IS HIVEGEAR?

Hivegear is a Spanish company founded in 2021 focused on the development and distribution of video games.

WHAT IS OUR GOAL?

We specifically specialize in the development of video games, web applications, databases, software...

Our most ambitious goal is to market and publicize our products around the world, creating a quality brand that consumers can trust.

WHAT ARE OUR IDEALS?

Our ideal is characterized by making quality video games that we would buy to be able to play them, until the product in question has not reached the necessary expectations for the launch, we will not stop developing and idealizing our vision of the game.

With this method we seek to create a successful product that earns good reviews and is enjoyed when playing it.

WHAT OUR INFRASTRUCTURES OFFER?

Hivegear is a well-known video game development company that has state-of-the-art facilities that cover a vast space of 2,000 square meters. Our headquarters is specifically designed to encourage creativity and collaboration among our talented team of professionals.

‍

Our facilities include modern and ergonomic workspaces, designed with the comfort and productivity of our developers in mind. We have dedicated areas for different departments such as game design, programming, art, and animation, where teams can focus on their tasks efficiently. Furthermore, at Hivegear we understand the importance of communication and teamwork.

That's why we've created communal spaces, such as meeting rooms and breakout areas, where our employees can collaboratively brainstorm, discuss concepts, and find creative solutions.

These spaces are equipped with the latest technology to facilitate presentations and brainstorming sessions. We have also dedicated a special area for the testing and quality department, where our experts make sure that each of our games meets the highest standards of performance, gameplay, and user experience.

We have a wide variety of equipment and game consoles to carry out extensive tests to ensure that our products are flawless. At Hivegear, we value the well-being of our team. For this reason, our facilities also include a rest and recreation area, where our employees can relax, enjoy a moment of recreation and recharge their batteries.

This space has comfortable seating areas, an equipped kitchen, and various entertainment options. Our facilities are located in an inspiring environment, surrounded by beautiful natural landscapes that provide an environment conducive to creativity. In addition, we are close to important technological and university centers, which allows us to keep up to date with the latest trends and establish strategic collaborations.

‍

We recommend you visit our facilities and learn first-hand about the exciting world of Hivegear! If you are interested in scheduling a visit or would like more information, please do not hesitate to contact us. We will be delighted to receive you and share our passion for video games with you.

ASTEROIDS ++

The objective of the game is to destroy all the asteroids that apper on the screen, acquiring scores and beating levels.

HIVEGEAR PRIVACY POLICY

Hivegear is committed to protecting the privacy of its users and complying with applicable data privacy laws and regulations.
This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect our users' personal information when they use our website.
‍
Information Collection
When users visit our website, we collect certain information automatically, such as the user's IP address, date, and time of access, the type of browser used and the pages visited. This information is collected anonymously and is used to improve the quality of our website and user experience. In addition, when users register with our website, we may collect personal information such as name, email address and contact information. We may also collect information about our users' gaming preferences and interests to improve our game offerings.

Use of information
The information we collect is used to improve the quality of our website and user experience, to offer new games and to inform users about games and services that might be of interest to them. We may also use the information to tailor targeted advertising and marketing to our users. Information Sharing Hivegear will not share your users' personal information with third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law or court order. However, we may share anonymous and aggregated information with third parties for statistical and marketing purposes.
‍
Protection of information
Hivegear is committed to protecting the personal information of its users and has implemented technical and organizational security measures to ensure data security and prevent unauthorized access.

Privacy Policy Changes
Hivegear reserves the right to change this privacy policy at any time. If we make significant changes in the way we collect, use or share personal information, we will notify users through the website or by email.
